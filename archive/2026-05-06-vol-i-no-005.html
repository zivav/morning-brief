CHAPTER II · AI TRADE · PARTNERSHIP DEPTH

META + OPENAI:
12GW MANDATE

AMD disclosed back-to-back 6GW commitments from Meta and OpenAI on the same earnings call — the largest simultaneous vendor-diversification signal in AI infrastructure history. Both deployments center on the custom Instinct MI450 GPU, co-engineered with Meta.

  • MI450 specs — 432 GB HBM4 memory, 19.6 TB/s bandwidth
  • Meta deal — 6GW multi-year; 160M AMD share warrant vesting on milestones; first 1GW H2 2026 on Helios rack architecture with EPYC "Venice" CPUs
  • OpenAI deal — 6GW strategic; 1GW initial MI450 deployment H2 2026; ROCm software stack as CUDA alternative
  • Server CPU share — AMD guided market growth >35%; ratio target 1:1 server CPU deployment
  • Key risk — ROCm ecosystem maturity vs. CUDA; warrant dilution 160M shares (~10% of float at current levels)
