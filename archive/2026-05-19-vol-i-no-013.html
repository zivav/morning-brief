FROM HORMUZ
TO BARAKAH

Drones struck the Barakah Nuclear Power Plant in UAE's Al Dhafra region on May 17 — the Arab world's only operational nuclear facility, supplying ~25% of UAE electricity. Two of three drones were intercepted; one ignited a fire in an external electrical generator outside the inner perimeter. IAEA confirmed radiation levels normal; all four reactors operational.

  • Attribution: UAE not officially blaming Iran; attack pattern matches prior Iranian drone operations against Gulf energy infrastructure
  • Brent reaction: Intraday spike to $111.50/bbl on May 18 — compounding the existing Hormuz closure premium
  • Iran MOU status: 14-point memo in negotiation; Tehran hardliners pushing escalation post-Barakah; Trump: "better get moving, FAST"
  • US military option: Pentagon briefing Trump on potential operations against Iran (Operation Sledgehammer contingency remains live)
  • IEA forecast: Global supply undersupplied through October; 4M bbl/day Hormuz flow loss
